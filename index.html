OUR WORK

Challenge Description!

Our team has developed a smart cane that is specifically designed to assist visually impaired individuals in exploring the world without any assistance. We recognize that many similar products are currently available in the market, but our objective is to make this technology accessible to everyone by reducing the price and complexity. Our smart cane is equipped with various features, including an inbuilt buzzer that reminds the person to take their medicine, fall detection technology, obstacle detection, a Google Maps Navigator, and physical fitness tracking. We have used a Raspberry Pi Zero 2 W at the core of the device, along with non-contact IR temperature sensors, heart rate and blood oxygen (pulse oximeter) sensors, and an optical distance sensor for obstacle detection. Our aim is to empower visually challenged individuals to live independently and safely, and we believe that our smart cane is a significant step in that direction.



Solution to the addressed probem!

The project aims to develop a Smart Cane to aid visually impaired individuals in navigating their surroundings independently. The Smart Cane incorporates various components to ensure the safety and well-being of the user. These include an ultrasonic sensor for obstacle detection, an accelerometer and gyroscope module for fall detection and triggering of an alert, crash detection through the accelerometer, GPS for navigation with voice assistance, an RTC module for medicine reminders, a fitness tracking module for temperature, heart rate, and blood oxygen level monitoring, a Bluetooth headset for communication, and a LIPO battery for power supply. The project activities involve designing and assembling the Smart Cane, integrating the various components and ensuring they function seamlessly, testing the device extensively for accuracy and reliability, and optimizing the design for accessibility and affordability. The output of the project is a functional and user-friendly Smart Cane that enables visually impaired individuals to navigate their surroundings independently and safely, while also providing reminders for medicine and monitoring their physical fitness.



Impact in the society!

The Smart Cane for blind people is a technological innovation that has a positive impact on society. It empowers and enables blind individuals to move around independently, ensuring their safety through features such as obstacle detection and GPS navigation. The device also incorporates fitness tracking, medication reminders, and a communication system that enables users to interact with the virtual assistant in noisy environments. The Smart Cane provides a valuable solution to a community challenge, improving the social values and quality of life for differently abled individuals while also promoting inclusivity and equal opportunities for all.
